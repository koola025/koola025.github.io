About Me

I am currently pursuing a B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering at National Tsing Hua University. This summer(2018), I have started doing research in vision science lab with Prof. Min Sun as my advisor. Since I entered college, I have joined Colde Garage (a maker space at NTHU EE), where I have built the app RunRRR with a group of awesome people and also found my interest in front-end development. I have built several mobile apps, web apps, computer games and some gadgets with Arduino since then.
